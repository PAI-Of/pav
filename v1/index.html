Legend




Explanation

Black = Power Positive
Green = Power Negative
Blue = Control Back
Red = Control Front
Magenta = Motor Negative
Orange = Motor Positive

When Blue is set to 1 (True), the circuit provides + from the right and – from the left to the motor, making it spin anti-clockwise (BACK).
When Red is 1, – from the right and + from the left are provided, spinning the motor clockwise (FRONT).

To connect:

  • Connect Orange to motor's +
  • Connect Magenta to motor's –
  • Connect Green to battery –
  • Connect Black to battery +
