Zero Fusion Technology

Our revolutionary energy transfer methods redefine what's possible in power delivery systems.

Type 1

Thermal Point Transfer (TPT)

Efficiency: ~80%

Send heat → captured by thermoelectric/thermal-PV modules

Type 2

Hydro Spin Launch (HSL)

Efficiency: ~40%

Spin water → transfer kinetic energy → captured by turbines/EMF

Type 3

Magnetic Displacement Transfer (MDT)

Efficiency: ~55%

Spinning/oscillating magnets induce current in receiver coil

Type 4

Particle Beam Charge (PBC)

Efficiency: ~30%

Fire plasma/ions → receiver captures electric flow
